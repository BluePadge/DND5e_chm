苏苏瓦魔 Shoosuva

（描述翻译暂缺）

“What? Are you expecting me to comment on these creatures? Fine, how's this: a loyal pet deserves a loyal pet.”

― Mordenkainen

“Trust Mordenkainen to look down on any sort of companionship―even the slavering, venomous, demonic puppy kind.”

― Tasha

A shoosuva is a hyena-demon gifted by Yeenoghu to an especially powerful worshiper (typically a fang of Yeenoghu). A shoosuva manifests shortly after a Yeenoghu-worshiping war band achieves a great victory, emerging from a billowing, fetid cloud of smoke as it arrives from the Abyss. In battle, the demon wraps its slavering jaws around one victim while lashing out with the poisonous stinger on its tail to bring down another. A creature immobilized by the poison becomes easy pickings for any nearby members of the war band.

Each shoosuva is bonded to a particular worshiper of Yeenoghu and fights alongside its master. A gnoll that has been gifted with a shoosuva is second only to a flind in status within a war band dedicated to Yeenoghu.

动作Actions
多重攻击Multiattack。苏苏瓦魔进行一次啃咬攻击与一次尾刺攻击。
啃咬Bite。近战武器攻击：命中+7，触及5尺，单一目标。命中：26（4d10+4）点穿刺伤害。
尾刺Tail Stinger。近战武器攻击：命中+7，触及15尺，单一生物。命中：13（2d8+4）点穿刺伤害，且目标必须成功通过一个DC14的体质豁免，否则便中毒。当因此而中毒时，目标同时被麻痹。目标在其每个回合结束时可以重复进行此豁免，豁免成功则终止其所承受的此效应。
附赠动作Bonus Actions
横行Rampage。当在自己的回合中以一次近战攻击将一个生物的生命值降至0时，苏苏瓦魔可以移动至多其速度一半的距离，并进行一次啃咬攻击。
